Olubobo Privacy Policy

Privacy First Game

Introduction

Welcome to Olubobo, a word scramble puzzle game developed by Grow Together Youth Alliance. Olubobo is created by a group of young developers showcasing their software development skills while building fun and educational digital experiences. This privacy policy explains how the application handles information.

Your Privacy Matters

Olubobo is designed with privacy in mind. The application does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information. You can enjoy the game without creating an account or providing any personal details.

Information We Collect

Olubobo does not collect any personal information. We do not collect:

  • Name or personal identity information
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location information
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Financial information
  • Device identifiers for tracking

Account Information

Olubobo does not require:

  • Account registration
  • Login credentials
  • User profiles

The game works immediately after installation.

Offline Data Storage

Olubobo stores game progress locally on your device. This includes information such as:

  • Highest score
  • Game statistics
  • Achievements
  • User preferences

This information remains on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

Internet Access

Olubobo is designed as an offline-first application. The game does not require an internet connection to function.

Permissions

Olubobo does not request any sensitive device permissions. The application does not access:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Storage files

Children and Young Users

Olubobo is designed to provide a safe and educational gaming experience. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

Third-Party Services

Olubobo does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies that collect user information.

Data Security

Because Olubobo does not collect personal information, there is no personal data stored on external servers. Local game data is protected by your device's security features.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy when necessary to reflect improvements to the application. Any changes will be published on this page.
